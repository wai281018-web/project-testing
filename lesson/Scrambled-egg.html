Scrambled Egg

A simple,delicious,and quick mean that anyone can cook at home.

Prep time:2 mins | Cook time:3 mins

Ingredients

  • eggs 2pics
  • milk1 table spoon
  • butter1 gea spoon
  • saltA little
  • Black pepperA little

Cooking Instructions

  1. Firstly,crack the eggs into a bowl and mix them.
  2. Add a little milk,salt and black pepper.
  3. Melt the butter in a pan over medium heat.
  4. pour the egg mixture into the pan and stir slowly.
  5. Cook until the eggs are soft and fluffy.
